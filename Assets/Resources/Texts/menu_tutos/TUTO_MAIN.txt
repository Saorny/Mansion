Welcome to the game!

You control a detective in charge of solving a mysterious case (see the tutorial section to known more about the background). You need to explore areas, collect objects and solve puzzles to follow up the story. Here are a few useful infos:

- press "W/A/S/D" : strife front/left/back/right

- press "i" : access inventory

- press "escape" : access menu, or close current menu

- "space" : jump

- "e" : collect or interact with an object
